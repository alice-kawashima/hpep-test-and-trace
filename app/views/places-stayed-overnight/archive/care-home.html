What happens next

Make sure you tell care home staff that you have tested positive for COVID-19.

Because you live in a care home, extra measures are in place to help keep you safe.

A Local Health Protection Team will contact your care home management and staff. Together they will make a plan to support and protect everyone who lives and works at the home. They will also help with contact tracing.
