NHS Test and Trace

This service will use the information you give us to trace your recent contacts. If you are under 18, you should ask your parent or legal guardian to complete this with you.

We'll also give you advice about self-isolation and where to find support.

Use this service if you:

  • have tested positive for COVID-19
  • are the parent or legal guardian of a child or young person under the age of 18 who has tested positive for COVID-19
  • are giving us information for someone aged 18 or over who has tested positive for COVID-19

Before you start

We will ask you about:

  • places visited recently, like places of work or education
  • names and contact details of people at those places

To help you remember these details, you might find it helpful to look at your calendars, social media, or the NHS COVID-19 app.

By giving us these details, you are helping to stop the spread of COVID-19.

If you are delayed or have problems completing this service online, a member of the NHS Test and Trace team will call you soon. You'll know it's us when you see a call from the number 0300 013 5000.

By using this service you are agreeing to our privacy policy.
