Trace close contacts after a positive test

Use this service if you tested positive for something that might put the health of others at risk, like:

  • an infectious illness
  • an exposure to a hazardous substance.

We will use the information you give us to trace people you've been in close contact with. We'll give them advice on how to stay safe. If they should get tested, we'll tell them how. We will not reveal your details to them.

Before you start

We will ask you about:

  • places you've been
  • names and contact details of people you've been with

Checking your calendars and social media may help you remember these details.

By using this service you are agreeing to our privacy policy.
